About Antahsagari

Antahsagari is a student-driven underwater ROV initiative under 4i-Labs, IIT Guwahati. It addresses underwater exploration challenges through engineering innovation.

Project Aim

  • Explore Brahmaputra's bed and ecosystem.
  • Inspect dams, pipelines and underwater structures.
  • Monitor water quality and species distribution.

Key Features

5 Thrusters
Raspberry Pi + Pixhawk
Leak & Pressure Sensors
Subsea Lights & Camera Tilt
